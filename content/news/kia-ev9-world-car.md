The Kia EV9 has been crowned World Car of the Year, beating a shortlist dominated by large electric SUVs. The three-row EV impressed judges with its combination of space, technology, and value.

<!--more-->

## A changing market

The win underscores how quickly the industry is moving toward family-sized electric vehicles. Where compact crossovers once led EV adoption, buyers are now demanding three-row practicality without the diesel bill.

Kia also took the World Electric Vehicle award, rounding out a strong showing for the brand's electric push. Rivals are already previewing competing models ahead of next year's cycle.
