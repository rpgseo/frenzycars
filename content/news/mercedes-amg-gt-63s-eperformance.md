Mercedes-AMG has crowned the new GT lineup with the 63 S E Performance, a plug-in hybrid flagship packing a combined 831 horsepower.

<!--more-->

## Hybrid, but make it fast

The 4.0-litre biturbo V8 drives the rear wheels, while a 201 HP electric motor on the rear axle fills the torque gaps. The result is a claimed 0-100 km/h sprint of 2.9 seconds and an electric-only range of around 13 kilometres.
