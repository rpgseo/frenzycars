The Hyundai Ioniq 5 N has been named Performance EV of the Year, beating finalists from Porsche, Kia, and BMW.

<!--more-->

Judges praised the car's N e-Shift system, which simulates eight-speed gear changes, and its N Grin Boost for 10-second bursts of peak power. At 641 horsepower, it blends theatrics with genuine pace — 0 to 100 km/h in 3.4 seconds.

A sign that performance EVs no longer have to be silent and serious.
