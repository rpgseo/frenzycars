Ford has confirmed the Mustang GTD is undertaking an official Nürburgring lap programme, with the goal of setting a production-car record. Early sector times reported by trackside observers suggest the supercharged V8 muscle car is genuinely quick.

<!--more-->

## Born from racing

The GTD borrows suspension logic from Ford's GT3 racing programme, including a multilink rear axle and active aerodynamics. It is the most extreme road-going Mustang ever built.

Ford has not published a time yet, but engineers are targeting sub-7-minute territory — territory normally reserved for European mid-engine exotics. A full announcement is expected later this season.
