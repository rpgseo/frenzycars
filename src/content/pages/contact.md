## Get in Touch

We are a small, independent team. We read every message that comes in.

**Editorial tips & story ideas**
Spotted something we should cover? Working on a car we have not reviewed? Drop us a line.

**Corrections**
We take accuracy seriously. If you spot an error — a wrong lap time, a misquoted spec, a bad calculation — please tell us and we will fix it promptly with an editorial note.

**Press & manufacturer enquiries**
We accept test vehicles for review on a strictly independent basis. We do not guarantee coverage in exchange for access, and editorial decisions remain entirely with our team.

**Reader mail**
We enjoy hearing from readers who disagree with us. It keeps us honest.

**Email:** [hello@frenzycars.com](mailto:hello@frenzycars.com)

We aim to respond within 48 hours on working days.
