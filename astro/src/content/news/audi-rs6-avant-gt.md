Audi has pulled the wraps off the RS6 Avant GT, a limited-run send-off for the C8 generation that turns the super-wagon up to eleven.

<!--more-->

## Lighter, sharper, rarer

The GT uses a carbon-fibre bonnet, fenders and doors to shed weight, while the 4.0-litre twin-turbo V8 is pushed to 621 horsepower. Quattro and a sport differential put the power down, and Audi claims 0-100 km/h in under 3.5 seconds.

Only 660 units will be produced worldwide, each hand-finished. Pricing is expected to sit well above the standard RS6 when order books open this autumn.
