Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences and gather anonymous usage data.

Cookies We Use

Analytics — Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how readers find and use FrenzyCars. GA4 collects anonymised data about pages visited, session duration, and traffic sources. No personally identifiable information is collected through GA4.

Google’s privacy policy applies to data collected via GA4. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising — Google AdSense

When advertising is active on FrenzyCars, Google AdSense and its advertising partners may place cookies on your device to serve interest-based ads based on your browsing activity across websites. These cookies are set by Google, not by FrenzyCars directly.

You can manage interest-based advertising preferences at Google’s Ad Settings.

Functional Cookies

We may use cookies to remember your preferences (such as display settings) and to keep the site functioning correctly. These cookies do not track you across other websites.

How to Control Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on FrenzyCars.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
