What is the best 3-row SUV for families?

Est. cost: $36000–$60000 USD

Quick answer

The Kia Telluride and Hyundai Palisade are the best 3-row SUVs for most families, offering the strongest blend of cabin space, safety, and value in the segment. For buyers who prioritize long-term reliability and fuel economy, the Toyota Highlander (especially the Hybrid) is the smart pick.

The best 3-row SUV for a family is the one that genuinely fits your daily life — not just the one with the highest towing rating. That means a cabin roomy enough for car seats, boosters, and the inevitable pile of backpacks, plus a third row a real adult or older child can actually use without climbing gymnastics.

Safety is non-negotiable for family hauling, so we weight NHTSA star ratings and IIHS Top Safety Pick status heavily. Beyond crash scores, look for easy LATCH anchor access for car seats, ample cargo volume behind the third row for strollers and groceries, and driver-assist tech like blind-spot monitoring and rear-seat reminder. Value matters too: a well-equipped mid-trim model often beats a loaded luxury SUV for the same money.

The picks below cover the full family spectrum — from the segment-favorite Kia Telluride and Hyundai Palisade, to the efficient Toyota Highlander Hybrid, the practical Honda Pilot, the upscale Mazda CX-90, and the cargo-hauling Chevrolet Traverse.

Frequently asked questions

What is the most reliable 3-row family SUV?

The Toyota Highlander is widely regarded as the most reliable 3-row family SUV, backed by Toyota long-term dependability data and strong resale value. The Honda Pilot also scores highly for long-term reliability among mainstream midsize 3-row SUVs.

Which 3-row SUV is best for car seats?

The Kia Telluride and Hyundai Palisade are the easiest SUVs for car seats thanks to three full sets of LATCH anchors with good access and a wide, flat second row. The Honda Pilot adds flexibility with a removable second-row middle seat that simplifies third-row access.

What is the best value 3-row SUV for families?

The Kia Telluride offers the best overall value, delivering near-luxury interior quality, top safety ratings, and abundant cargo for under $40,000 in well-equipped trims. The Hyundai Palisade is a close second with similar pricing and a plusher cabin.

What is the safest 3-row SUV?

The Hyundai Palisade and Mazda CX-90 earn IIHS Top Safety Pick+ ratings, the highest available, along with NHTSA 5-Star ratings. The Kia Telluride, Toyota Highlander, and Honda Pilot all carry 5-Star NHTSA ratings and IIHS Top Safety Pick status, making the segment as a whole very safe.

Is a hybrid 3-row SUV worth it for families?

Yes, if you drive enough to offset the higher purchase price. The Toyota Highlander Hybrid returns up to 36 mpg combined — roughly double a conventional V6 — which can save thousands in fuel over a typical family ownership period. It is the strongest fuel-economy case in the 3-row segment.
