Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

FrenzyCars (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who reads our site. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • Newsletter subscription: Your email address when you sign up for The Frenzy Bulletin.
  • Contact form: Your name, email address, and message when you contact us.

Data collected automatically

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised data about site usage — pages visited, time on page, browser type, approximate geographic location (country/region), and traffic source. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for full details.

How We Use Your Data

Purpose Data used
Sending the newsletter Email address
Responding to enquiries Name, email address
Improving site content Anonymised analytics data
Serving relevant advertising Cookie data via Google AdSense (when active)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties.

We share data with the following service providers solely to operate the site:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymised traffic analytics
  • Google AdSense — advertising (when active)
  • Email provider — newsletter delivery

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

Data Retention

Newsletter subscribers: retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form enquiries: retained for up to 12 months. Analytics data: subject to Google’s standard retention settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@frenzycars.com.

Security

We use SSL encryption for all data transmitted to and from FrenzyCars. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, though no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

FrenzyCars is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We will post any changes to this policy on this page with an updated date. For material changes, we will notify newsletter subscribers by email.

Contact

Privacy enquiries: hello@frenzycars.com
