Our Mission

Why FrenzyCars Exists

Car media has a credibility problem. Press junkets, manufacturer-funded test drives, and affiliate-padded buying guides have blurred the line between journalism and advertising. FrenzyCars was built to fix that.

We are an independent automotive editorial magazine. We answer to readers — not PR departments, not advertisers, not OEMs.

What We Stand For

Honest testing over access. If a car is bad, we say so — even if it means fewer invitations to product launches. Track times, road feel, and real-world range matter more than manufacturer spec sheets.

Depth over volume. We would rather publish one definitive review than ten rushed takes recycled from a press release. Every article is reported, driven, and written by people who think about cars constantly.

Independence above all. FrenzyCars is reader-supported. We may earn affiliate commissions on some recommended products (disclosed clearly), but editorial decisions are never influenced by commercial relationships.

Who We Write For

You already know more than most. You know the difference between a torque curve and a power band. You have opinions about tyre compounds and heel-and-toe downshifts. You did not come here to be told a crossover SUV is sporty because it has red brake calipers.

FrenzyCars is written for enthusiasts who demand precision — and enjoy reading about it.
