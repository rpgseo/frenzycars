About FrenzyCars

FrenzyCars is an independent editorial magazine for people who care about cars — not just as transport, but as objects of obsession.

Founded in 2026, we publish instrument-tested reviews, breaking news, and long-form features. We answer to readers, not advertisers. Every car we praise has earned it.

  • No content written from a press release alone
